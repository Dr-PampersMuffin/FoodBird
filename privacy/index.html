Privacy Policy – Food Bird

Last updated:

Food Bird (“we”, “our”, “us”) aggregates publicly available restaurant information to help you discover and compare delivery options. We do not sell personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Account info: name, email (if you sign in)
  • Usage data: app interactions, device type
  • Optional location: to show nearby restaurants (with your permission)

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve app features
  • Show relevant restaurants and offers
  • Prevent fraud and troubleshoot

Data Sharing

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal requests as required by law

We do not sell your personal data.

Data Retention & Security

We retain data only as long as necessary and use reasonable safeguards. No method is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion at: info@foodbird.com
  • You can disable location in device settings.

Children

Food Bird is not intended for children under 13.
