
Instructions to install FoodBird_v1.ipa:

Option 1 – Apple Configurator 2 (Mac):
1. Download from the Mac App Store.
2. Connect your iPhone XR via USB.
3. Open Configurator, drag in 'FoodBird_v1.ipa'.
4. Follow on-screen prompts.

Option 2 – AltStore (Mac/Windows):
1. Go to https://altstore.io and install AltServer.
2. Connect your iPhone via USB.
3. Install AltStore on your phone.
4. Open AltStore on your iPhone and import 'FoodBird_v1.ipa'.

You're ready to test Food Bird on your iPhone!
